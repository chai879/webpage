Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is one of the most famous monuments in the world. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal and is considered a masterpiece of Mughal architecture.

Constructed in the 17th century, the Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attracts millions of visitors each year. Its white marble structure, intricate carvings, and beautiful gardens make it a symbol of eternal love.
